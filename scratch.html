Fun Facts

  1. Common Elephant Facts:
    • Elephants are the world's largest land animal
    • Elephants are herbivores
    • They are mostly found in Africa and Asia
  2. Uncommon Elephant Facts:
    • An elephants trunk can hold up to 2.5 gallons of water
    • The Elephant is the national animal of Thailand
    • Elephants purr like cats do as a form of communication
